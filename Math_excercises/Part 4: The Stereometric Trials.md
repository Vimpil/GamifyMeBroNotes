## Volume of a Truncated Pyramid

The formula for the volume of a truncated pyramid is:

$\[ \text{Volume} = \frac{1}{3} \left( a^2 + b^2 + ab \right) \]$

where:
- $\( a \)$ is the side length of the bottom base
- $\( b \)$ is the side length of the top base
